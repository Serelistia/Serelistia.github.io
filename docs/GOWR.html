DIGIT 210 Project: God of War: Ragnarök

Partial Game Transcript Analysis

Spring 2026

Introduction

God of War: Ragnarök (2022) is an action-adventure game by Santa Monica Studio, initially released worldwide on 11 Nov 2022 for the Playstation 4 and Playstation 5 before receiving a PC port on 20 Sep 2024. It follows the titular God of War, Kratos, and his son Atreus, on the continuation of their journeys in the Norse realm.

The Partial Transcript

This project will analyze the game transcript from the beginning of the game, up to the ending of THE PATH: The Lost Sanctuary.
